Values & Vision

I am a fervent believer in regeneration and decentralization. As a co-founder of EthicHub, I work at the intersection of ReFi and real-world impact, channeling financing to small coffee producers across six countries.

My work is driven by a passion for tackling climate change, biodiversity loss, and global inequities. I advocate for democratizing opportunities in education and funding to unlock talent everywhere, regardless of geography.

"Brilliant people should be the first leading de-escalation! There shouldn't be two sides but a wide range of positions."

I reject polarized extremes in favor of rational, cosmopolitan dialogue. I believe that blockchain and technology are not just financial tools, but bridges to create a less fractured world.
